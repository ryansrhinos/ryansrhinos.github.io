About Me

Welcome to my portfolio website!

I'm Rahul, a passionate and driven individual with a diverse skill set in engineering and management. I'm a Mechanical Engineering graduate with a minor in Business Management and a Master's in Manufacturing Engineering. My academics and work experience has equipped me with a unique blend of technical and managerial expertise.

As you explore this website, you'll gain insights into my exciting projects, industry experiences, and education. Based in the lively city of Toronto, Ontario, I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to make a positive impact. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy reading about my projects and browsing through my photos.
